Basic Formulas

Train a km/hr to m/s: (a*5/18) m/s a m/s to km/hr: (a*18/5) km/hr Train is moving in same direction then relative speed: (u-v)m/s Train is moving in opposite direction then relative speed: (u+v)m/s Two trains of legth a meteres and b meteres are moving in opposite directions at u m/s and v m/s: (a+b)/(u+v) sec Two trains of legth a meteres and b meteres are moving in same directions at u m/s and v m/s: (a+b)/(u-v) sec
Simple Interest SI=(P*T*R)/100 P=(100*SI)/(R*T) R=(100*SI)/(P*T) T=(100*SI)/(P*R)
Compound Interest Amount=P(1+R/100)^n Amount=P(1+(R/2)/100)^2n Amount=P(1+(R/4)/100)^4n
Profit and Loss Profit=(SP)-(CP) Loss=(CP)-(SP) Profit %=(Profit*100)/CP Loss %=(Loss*100)/CP
Average Average= (Sum of observations)/ (Number of observations)
Alligation (Quantity of cheaper/ Quantity of dearer)= (CP of dearer- Mean Price)/(Mean price- CP of cheaper)
Percentage Percentage increase= (R/(100+R)*100)% Percentage decrease= (R/(100-R)*100)% Population after n years= P(1+R/100)^n Population n years ago= P/(1+R/100)^n
Boats and Strams Downstream=(u+v) km/hr Upstream=(u-v) km/hr Speed in still water= 1/2(a+b) km/hr Rate of stream= 1/2(a-b) km/hr
General formulas (a+b)(a-b)=(a^2-b^2) (a+b)^2=(a^2+b^2+2ab) (a-b)^2=(a^2+b^2-2ab) (a^3+b^3)=(a+b)(a^2-ab+ b^2) (a^3-b^3)=(a-b)(a^2+ab+b^2)
Speed Time and Distance Speed=(Distance/Time) Time=(Distance/Speed) Distance=(Speed*Time)
